Pizza

This is a margarita cooked in napolitan style. Following is a step by step tutorial to cook this delicious pizza.

Ingredient

    Dough: 500 grams of Tipo "00" flour 325 ml of water 10 grams of sea salt 3 grams of fresh yeast or 1 gram of active dry yeast
    Sauce: 400 grams of canned San Marzano tomatoes (preferably DOP) A pinch of sea salt
    Toppings: 200 grams of fresh mozzarella cheese (preferably buffalo mozzarella or fior di latte) Fresh basil leaves Extra virgin olive oil

Preparation

  1. the Dough: he flour and salt in a large bowl. Dissolve the yeast in the water, then add to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for about 10-15 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise at room temperature for 8-24 hours, or until it has doubled in size. Divide the dough into 2-4 equal portions, shape each into a ball, and let them rest for another 2-4 hours. Prepare the Sauce: Crush the San Marzano tomatoes by hand or use a food mill to achieve a smooth consistency. Add a pinch of sea salt to taste.
  2. our oven to its highest setting, ideally 250°C (482°F) or higher, with a pizza stone inside. Stretch one dough ball into a 10-12 inch round on a floured surface. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Tear the mozzarella into small pieces and distribute evenly over the sauce. Add fresh basil leaves
  3. Frizzle a bit of extra virgin olive oil over the pizza. Transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until the crust is charred and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  4. Remove the pizza from the oven, add a few more fresh basil leaves if desired, slice, and serve immediately.
